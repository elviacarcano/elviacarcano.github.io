mocla

branding & product design

For this piece, I was asked to create a product based off of the insect, the Witchetty Grub. Our product had to be edible and had to either feature the insect as the sole food or use it as an ingredient. For my product, I chose to create mazapanes, which are a traditional Mexican peanut candy, using my insect as an ingredient.
