body positivity

artwork

For this piece, I was asked to create a composition only using the colors black, white, and gray. The design also had to be based off of a set of opposites, so I decided to focus on inside and out. Since body positivity is something I strongly support, I decided to create a woman embracing her stretch marks. This is meant to illustrate that the way others view a person does not always reflect how they view themselves.
